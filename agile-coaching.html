What is agile coaching?

Sports teams have coaches to help them be better.  An agile coach is a sports coach for organizations.

Agile coaching provides temporary (or permanent) guidance to a team or the entire organization to help you be more effective, efficient, and ultimately respond faster to change while delivery value consistently.  

The primary goal of an agile coach is to make teams awesome and create products and services that customers love.
